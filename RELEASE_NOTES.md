# Stellar Clipboard v2.0.6

## 中文

外观修复。

- **图标尺寸对齐 macOS 规范**:此前图标内容占满整个画布,在 Dock 中
  显得比相邻应用大一圈。现在按 Apple 图标网格四周留出 ~10% 透明边距
  (内容占画布约 80%),Dock、程序坞与其他应用大小一致;窗口与托盘
  图标同步更新以保持一致。

从 v2.0.5 升级为全自动流程。

---

## English

Cosmetic fix.

- **Icon sizing now follows the macOS grid**: the icon artwork used to
  fill the entire canvas, making it look larger than neighboring apps in
  the Dock. It now leaves the standard ~10% transparent margin (artwork
  ~80% of the canvas) per Apple's icon grid, matching other apps; window
  and tray icons were updated to match.

Upgrading from v2.0.5 uses the fully automatic flow.
